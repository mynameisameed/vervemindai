# Why MedSpas Are Losing $140K in Dormant Revenue

The average MedSpa has **2,400 patients** in its database. But only 30-40% are "active" — meaning they've booked or visited in the last 6 months. The rest? They're **dormant assets** — patients who once trusted you, paid you, and loved your results.

They didn't leave because of a bad experience. They simply forgot.

## The Math That Should Keep You Up at Night

Let's run the numbers for a typical mid-market MedSpa:

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Total patient database | 2,400 |
| Active patients (visited in 6 months) | 840 (35%) |
| Dormant patients | 1,560 (65%) |
| Average treatment value | $450 |
| Expected reactivation rate (with AI outreach) | 12-18% |
| **Recoverable revenue** | **$84,240 – $126,360** |

Add in the upsell potential — a patient who comes back for Botox often adds lip filler or a hydrafacial — and you're easily looking at **$140K+ in recoverable lifetime value** sitting untouched in your PMS.

## Why Traditional Reactivation Fails

Most MedSpas try one of three approaches:

1. **Blast email campaigns** — 8-12% open rates, feels spammy, low conversion
2. **Front-desk callbacks** — Staff is already overwhelmed; callbacks fall to the bottom of the priority list
3. **Direct mail** — Expensive ($2-4/piece), slow, impossible to track ROI

The common thread? **None of these are conversational.** They're one-directional broadcasts that patients can easily ignore.
