## Why Indian SMBs Need AI Automation

Indian local businesses face a uniquely difficult challenge: leads pour in through multiple channels (WhatsApp, Justdial, Google, and direct calls) — but most small teams can only respond to a fraction before response-time windows close and the lead goes cold.

Research shows:
- **78% of Indian leads** expect a response within 5 minutes of inquiry
- **WhatsApp** is the primary communication channel for 60%+ of SMB leads
- **90% of prospects** who don't get a callback within 1 hour call a competitor

VerveMind solves this with a single automation hub.

## What the India Stack Includes

### WhatsApp Business API Integration
Every inbound lead from JustDial, Google, or a web form immediately receives a personalized WhatsApp message in Telugu, Hindi, or English — based on the caller's location or preference.

> "Hi! Thanks for your inquiry about our dental services. Are you looking to book a cleaning or a consultation? Reply 1 for Cleaning, 2 for Consultation, or just type your question."

### CRM Setup (Zoho / Kylas)
All leads are automatically pushed into Zoho CRM or Kylas with full source attribution, conversation history, and appointment status synced in real-time.

### Multilingual AI Agent
The AI is trained on Telugu and Hindi phrasing patterns for: greeting customs, pricing negotiations, appointment confirmations, and review requests.
