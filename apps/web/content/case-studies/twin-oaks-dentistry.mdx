# The Call Overflow Crisis

Dr. Sarah Lin operates Twin Oaks Family Dentistry, a mid-sized clinic with three hygienists and a front desk team of two. 

Despite having excellent staff, the clinic was experiencing a massive bottleneck: **the phones.**

Between processing insurance for check-outs, welcoming incoming patients, and answering routine questions, **38% of incoming calls were going to voicemail.** Even worse, 100% of after-hours and weekend calls were completely unmonitored. 

When a patient with an emergency toothache calls on a Saturday morning, they don't wait until Monday for a callback—they call the next dentist on Google.

## The Autonomous Solution

Twin Oaks integrated **VerveMind's AI Receptionist** to act as a 24/7 overflow agent.

- **During Business Hours:** The phone rings at the front desk twice. If the staff is busy checking out a patient, the AI catches the call on the 3rd ring.
- **After Hours:** The AI instantly picks up, qualifying emergencies and booking routine cleanings.
- **Dormant Reactivation:** The clinic exported a CSV of 340 dormant patients (no visit in 18+ months) and launched an outbound SMS/Call sequence.

## The 14-Day Results

Within the first two weeks of deployment, the AI handled 127 calls perfectly.

Because the AI has direct integrations with the OpenDental calendar, it booked **23 recall appointments** without a single human having to verify the schedule. 

Furthermore, the AI caught 4 after-hours emergency calls, successfully dispatching them for Monday morning priority slots, saving an estimated **$3,500 in immediate lost LTV**.
