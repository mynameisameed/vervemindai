Why this matters for VerveMind's audience

👨‍⚕️Your buyers are dentists, medspa owners, and HVAC operators — not tech founders. Heavy glassmorphism and blobs read as "startup demo" to them, not "reliable business tool."
📱Moving backgrounds burn GPU on mobile and older MacBooks. A dentist checking your site on Safari can hit visible lag — that kills trust immediately.
✅Recommendation: Option C — keep the dark premium feel, use the subtle top glow only on the hero section, animate only things that are genuinely "live" (the status dot, the call feed). Everything else: static, sharp, fast.
